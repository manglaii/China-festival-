Tie-dye



Tie-dye Is an ancient Chinese textile technology, technology heritage so far, with a strong national cultural characteristics, make it a set of art, abstraction and practical in one of the crafts, exudes a unique artistic charm, both high Art appreciation value, but also a strong practical value.

Raw materials used in natural linen, cotton, silk. Dye with Isatis root , safflower, water Coriaria, blackhead grass and other plants, all by hand-refined. simple and elegant, both with rich national characteristics, but also in line with modern aesthetic aesthetic. Its variety, color, style, beautiful; including all kinds of clothing, fabrics, bedding, tablecloths, curtains, wall, towels, coasters and other characteristics of handicrafts and household items.
